Core Components

Material Theme closely specifies the HTML markup for its core components. Blazor MDC implements many of these and aheres rigorously to the markup specification. We term these "Core Components. Blazor MDC also has non core Plus Components.

Component List

Component Notes
MTButton A Material Button.
MTCard A Material Card.
MTCheckbox A Material Checkbox. Implements a two state on/off checkbox, but not yet an indeterminate variety.
MTCircularProgress A Material Circular Progress indicator.
MTDataTable A Material Data Table without row selection.
MTDialog A Material Dialog. Can set scrim and escape button actions.
MTDrawer A Material Drawer.
MTIconButton A Material Icon Button.
MTIconButtonToggle A toggle variant of the Material Icon Button.
MTLinearProgress A Material Linear Progress bar.
MTList A Material List. Implements Material Theme Web Components one and two line lists, plus a BlazorMdc interpretation of a three line list.
MTMenu A Material Menu. Does not implement sub menus. May redesign parameterization.
MTRadioButton A Material Radio Button.
MTSelect A Material Select Menu.
MTSwitch A Material Switch.
MTTabBar A Material Tab Bar.
MTTextArea A Material Text Field expressed as a text area.
MTTextField A Material Text Field.
MTTooltip A Material Tooltip.
MTTopAppBar A Material Top App Bar.
